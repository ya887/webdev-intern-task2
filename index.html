FAQ

Question: How do I purchase tickets for the festival??

Answer: You can purchase tickets directly from our website by clicking on the "Get Tickets" button on the home page. Alternatively, you can also buy tickets at the entrance of the event venue.

Question: Are children allowed at the festival?

Answer: Yes, children are welcome to attend the festival. However, please note that children under the age of 12 must be accompanied by an adult at all times.

Question: Can I bring outside food and drinks to the festival?

Answer: Outside food and drinks are not permitted inside the festival grounds. However, we have a wide range of food and beverage vendors offering delicious options for you to enjoy.

Question: Is there parking available at the festival?

Answer: Yes, there is parking available near the festival venue. We recommend arriving early to secure a parking spot, as it can get crowded during peak hours.

Question: What happens if it rains during the festival?

Answer: The festival will proceed rain or shine. In the event of rain, we have covered areas and tents set up to provide shelter for attendees. We also advise bringing appropriate clothing and footwear for varying weather conditions.

Question: Are pets allowed at the festival?

Answer: With the exception of service animals, pets are not allowed at the festival. This policy ensures the safety and comfort of all attendees, including those with allergies or phobias.
